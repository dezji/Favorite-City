Why Raleigh Is My Favorite Town

Raleigh is my favorite town because of its hospitality, its beautiful nature and because I grew up there.

Hospitality

Don't let the "North" in North Carolina fool you, Raleigh is very much a southern city. There's an old saying about southern hospitality, and Raleigh fits the bill completely. Wherever you go, if you're in the city of Raleigh, you will never be far from a comforting smile or a polite "how do you do?" Even if you're having a bad day, or a bad day is trying to have you, the habitants of Raleigh will have you in high spirits in no time.

Childhood

Raleigh will always have a place in my heart because I spent half of my life there. Every memory I have from my childhood in Raleigh is a happy one. From the nightly drives through the pretty city, to the warm days of summer at the park.

Nature

Everything about outside in Raleigh is like something out of a magazine. From the beautiful city buildings to the spacious parks and walking trails. Even the breeze is fresh as if you were breathing on top of a mountain. Raleigh is truly beautiful inside and out.

Deziallia Morris | Seminole State
